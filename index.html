3U CubeSat

SPACE HAUC will be 34 cm x 10 cm x 10 cm - small enough to fit inside of a backpack. These dimensions conform to the CubeSat Design Specification (CDS) definition of a three-unit (3U) CubeSat.

group

Patch Antenna Array

On one of the 10 cm x 10 cm CubeSat faces, we will have an custom patch antenna array. It will have 16 radiating elements and will be an aperture-coupled stacked-patch design. Copper will be the patch antenna's conductive material along with Rogers PCB serving as the dielectric and other conductive materials acting as vias for the digital phasers.

settings

Beam Steering

By varying the phase of each signal sent to each of the patch antenna elements, we can take advantage of interference, both constructive and destructive, to "steer" the resultant antenna beam to a desired angle relative to the satellite face. This effect, also known as "beam forming," also increases the gain, and thus, the overall data rate. We expect SPACE HAUC to achieve upwards of 50 Megabits per second.



SPACE HAUC

We are 50+ undergraduates at the University of Massachusetts Lowell. We come from multiple science, engineering, and business majors. It's a big, multidisciplinary project that is focused on educating the next generation of space scientists, technologists, and policy makers.
